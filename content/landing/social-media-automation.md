## Stop posting. Start orchestrating.

Most teams spend their best hours pushing buttons across five apps. Petit treats every channel as one workflow — write once, schedule everywhere, and let the system handle the follow-up.

![Cross-channel calendar](calendar.png)

## What's in the box

- **One calendar, every channel.** Drag a post across LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Threads, and TikTok. Petit resizes, retimes, and respects each platform's quirks.
- **Reply queues that feel human.** Aggregate comments, DMs, and mentions into a single inbox. Reply with shortcuts and saved tones.
- **Reporting that earns its keep.** Auto-rolled weekly summaries you can forward to a client without editing.

## Built for small, fast teams

Petit assumes you don't have a social ops manager. The defaults are sane, the keyboard shortcuts are aggressive, and the AI suggestions are quiet until you ask.

- No "AI co-pilot" pop-ups.

## How it works

1. **Connect your channels** in 90 seconds with OAuth.
2. **Draft a post** in our editor — variants for every platform get generated automatically.
3. **Approve the schedule** Petit proposes, or override anything.
4. **Read the Monday digest** instead of compiling it.
