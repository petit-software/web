## How it works

1. **Connect your channels** in 90 seconds with OAuth.
2. **Draft a post** in our editor — variants for every platform get generated automatically.
3. **Approve the schedule** Petit proposes, or override anything.
4. **Read the Monday digest** instead of compiling it.
